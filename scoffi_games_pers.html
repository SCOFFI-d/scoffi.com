PERS: Ancient Escape
















About

PERS is a Puzzle - Escape Room game in which two archaeologists investigate the secrets of an ancient temple and try to escape from there. The temple includes various of puzzle rooms and a final arena with Shooter concept!

As the "En Garde" team, we made this game as our second college project in 2025. We were 5 members and I was the Game Designer, Game Developer and Sound Designer of the team.
