Blade of Vengeance
















About

Blade of Vengeance is a 1v1 combat game about Erina, a Japanese woman who lost her father as a child and travels to France to seek revenge through fencing. It features two modes: Campaign as a story mode, and Arcade, where two players can play against each other!

As the "En Garde" team, we made this game as our first college project in 2024. We were 3 members and I was the Game Designer and Project Manager of the team.
